# Track: News Japanese 📰

> *"NHK is one of the world's great public broadcasters. Reading it in Japanese — really reading it, not running it through Google Translate — is one of the most satisfying milestones in Japanese learning."*

News Japanese (ニュース日本語) is written Japanese at its most formal, dense, and politically neutral. It's the gold standard of standard language — no dialect, no slang, and a very specific set of grammatical structures and vocabulary that appear nowhere else. This track gets you reading NHK, understanding news broadcasts, and navigating written Japanese media.

---

## Why News Japanese Is Different

News Japanese has distinctive features that separate it from everyday speech and even from business writing:

1. **Heavy passive voice:** Events happen without people being named as agents
2. **Nominalizations:** Actions turned into noun phrases
3. **Formal conjunctions:** Classical-influenced connectors
4. **Topic-specific vocabulary:** Politics, economy, disasters, international affairs
5. **Dense information packing:** Japanese news sentences are long and information-rich
6. **Abbreviated forms:** Agency names, organization names, and titles compressed

## News Grammar Patterns

These appear constantly in news writing and almost nowhere else:

| Pattern | Example | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 〜によると | 警察によると... | "According to ~" |
| 〜として | 関係者として... | "In the capacity of / as ~" |
| 〜をめぐって | 議論をめぐって... | "Surrounding the issue of ~" |
| 〜において | 会議において... | "At / in the context of ~" |
| 〜に対し | 政府に対し... | "Toward / regarding ~" |
| 〜を受けて | 批判を受けて... | "In response to ~" |
| 〜にもかかわらず | 反対にもかかわらず... | "Despite ~" |
| 〜に加え | さらに加え... | "In addition to ~" |
| 〜を通じて | 交渉を通じて... | "Through ~ / via ~" |
| 〜とみられる | 原因とみられる... | "Is believed to be ~" (journalist hedge) |
| 〜もようだ | 合意もようだ | "Appears to be ~" (hedge) |
| 〜との見方もある | 悪化との見方もある | "There is also a view that ~" |
| 〜が明らかになった | 事実が明らかになった | "It has become clear that ~" |
| 〜とのことだ | 続けるとのことだ | "It is said that ~" (reported speech) |

## Passive Voice in News

News Japanese uses passive voice to:
- Avoid naming who did something
- Focus on the event rather than the agent
- Sound neutral and authoritative

| Active (spoken) | Passive (news style) | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 首相が発表した | 発表された | Was announced |
| 警察が逮捕した | 逮捕された | Was arrested |
| 政府が決定した | 決定された | Was decided |
| 会社が解雇した | 解雇された | Was dismissed |
| 裁判所が判決を下した | 判決が下された | A ruling was handed down |

## Reading a News Headline

Headlines compress language dramatically. Examples:

**Headline:** 首相、増税案を撤回へ　野党の反発受け
**Unpacked:** 首相が増税案を撤回する方向へ。野党からの反発を受けたため。
**Translation:** Prime Minister to withdraw tax increase proposal, following opposition pushback.

**Headline:** 大手企業、賃上げ率過去最高　春闘で妥結
**Unpacked:** 大手企業が、春季労使交渉（春闘）において、賃上げ率が過去最高水準で妥結した。
**Translation:** Major companies reach wage increase agreement at record rates in spring labor negotiations.

Key headline features:
- Subject often dropped (spring wage negotiation "結果" is implied)
- Particles dropped (〜が, 〜を commonly omitted)
- Verbs often replaced by nouns (〜へ instead of 〜するだろう)
- Abbreviated organization/event names (春闘 = spring labor struggle)

## Reading NHK: Step by Step

### Sample Sentence Analysis

**Original:**
> 政府は、少子化対策の強化に向けて、来月から新たな支援金制度を導入することを決定したと発表した。

**Breaking it down:**
- 政府は — As for the government (topic)
- 少子化対策の強化に向けて — toward strengthening measures against the declining birthrate
- 来月から — starting next month
- 新たな支援金制度を — a new financial support system [object]
- 導入することを決定したと — decided to introduce [indirect report]
- 発表した — announced

**Translation:**
"The government announced that it has decided to introduce a new financial support system starting next month, aimed at strengthening measures against the declining birthrate."

## Listening: NHK Radio and Television

### NHK Radio Japanese
- **NHKラジオニュース** — formal news broadcasts, excellent for pitch accent study
- Newscasters speak at ~280 characters per minute — fast, clear, standard accent
- Available as podcast and streaming at [nhk.or.jp](https://www.nhk.or.jp/radio/)

### NHK Television
- **NHKニュース7** (7pm) — main evening news
- **おはよう日本** — morning news program
- **クローズアップ現代** — documentary news program
- **日曜討論** — political debate program (extremely difficult, very rewarding)

### NHK for School (NHK for School)
Educational content for school children — accessible, well-produced, good intermediate listening.
